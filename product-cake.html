Cake Bag

Elegant, sturdy bags designed for bakeries and gifts

Cake bags are specially designed box-style bags with a flat bottom and side gussets, making them ideal for carrying cakes, pastries, and boxed items. The structured shape prevents contents from shifting during transport, making them the preferred choice for bakeries, confectioneries, and gift shops.
