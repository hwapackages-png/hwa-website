WHO WE ARE

Company Profile

HWA Packages (Private) Limited is a leading manufacturer and supplier of premium quality non-woven bags based in Pakistan. Incorporated under the Companies Act, 2017, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the eco-friendly packaging industry, serving over 500 businesses across retail, food, fashion, confectionery, and corporate sectors.

Manufacturing Excellence

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with 10+ production lines capable of producing high volumes without compromising on quality. From fabric cutting and printing to stitching and finishing, every stage is handled in-house with precision machinery and skilled craftsmanship.

Product Range

We manufacture a comprehensive range of non-woven bags including W-Cut, D-Cut, Loop Handle, Stitch, Cake, and Pouch bags. Each product is available in custom sizes, multiple GSM options (30–80), and up to three-color printing to match your brand identity.

Quality Assurance

Quality is the backbone of everything we do. Our bags undergo rigorous inspection at every stage — from raw material sourcing to final packaging. We use only premium non-woven polypropylene fabric that ensures durability, tear resistance, and a professional finish that elevates your brand.

Pan-Pakistan Reach

While our manufacturing facility operates from a centralized location, our distribution network spans across Pakistan. We serve businesses in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Faisalabad, and beyond — delivering on time with reliable logistics and nationwide shipping support.

Client-Centric Approach

From first inquiry to final delivery, we work closely with every client. Whether you need 50 kg of plain bags or a fully customized printed order, our team provides personalized guidance, competitive pricing, and flexible minimum order quantities to suit businesses of all sizes.

Sustainability Commitment

Every bag we produce is a step towards reducing plastic waste. Our non-woven bags are reusable, recyclable, and biodegradable — offering an eco-conscious alternative without compromising on strength or aesthetics. We believe in responsible manufacturing that benefits both business and the environment.

OUR RESPONSIBILITY

Social Responsibility

At HWA Packages, we believe that a successful business must give back to the community and the environment it operates in. Our commitment to social responsibility is not just a policy — it is woven into the very fabric of our operations, our products, and our people.

Fighting Plastic Pollution

Single-use plastic bags are one of the largest contributors to environmental pollution. Every non-woven bag we produce replaces hundreds of plastic bags over its lifetime. By choosing HWA Packages, our clients actively participate in reducing plastic waste from landfills, oceans, and waterways across Pakistan.

Employment and Skill Development

We provide stable employment to a growing workforce, with a strong focus on hiring from local communities. Our team receives hands-on training in machine operation, quality control, and production management — equipping them with skills that build long-term career growth and financial stability for their families.

Sustainable Manufacturing

Our production processes are designed to minimize waste and energy consumption. We optimize fabric cutting patterns to reduce material waste, recycle production offcuts, and continuously invest in energy-efficient machinery. Our goal is to ensure that the environmental footprint of making the bag is as small as the footprint it helps eliminate.

Community Welfare

We believe in building strong communities. HWA Packages supports local education initiatives, participates in environmental awareness campaigns, and contributes to community welfare programs. We actively encourage our clients and partners to join us in creating a cleaner, healthier Pakistan for future generations.

Ethical Business Practices

Transparency, fair pricing, and honest dealings are the cornerstones of our business relationships. We maintain ethical standards across our supply chain — from sourcing raw materials from responsible suppliers to ensuring fair wages and safe working conditions for every member of our team.
